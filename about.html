About

Hi, I'm Henrik. This site is a mix between keeping track of what I do and then writing about the stuff that pops into my head.

I work as a solution architect at knowit.dk. My responsibilities seem to be anything from consulting on enterprise-, solution- and application architecture, to working with technical project management, tech leadership and mentoring, and helping teams and clients get on board with AI.

Previously, I was an assistant professor in Computer Science, at Aarhus University. My main research focus was in the field of Human-Computer Interaction, with a special interest in collaborative computing around data and interfaces.

I have an MSc in Information Technology on Interaction Design, Open Data, and Smart City technologies. I hold a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Aarhus University in Human-Computer Interaction (Interaction Design, User Experience, User-centered design, and design research). My thesis focuses on the theoretical and empirical foundations for location-based services and community software.
